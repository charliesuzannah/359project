FUNsettling Fact #1

The US counts a “militant” as any man of military age in a “strike zone”.

FUNsettling Fact #2

The US does not account for population density, residential areas, etc. that a drone could not see from above.

FUNsettling Fact #3

The US sometimes bombs first responders helping victims of an initial drone strike which is a war crimes.

FUNsettling Fact #4

Very few of the people the US military have killed with drones are high-ranking al-Qaeda members.

FUNsettling Fact #5

At least two of our strikes in Yemen violated international law because the strikes "struck only civilians".
